Problem Statement

There is not enough parking on campus for all students and staff.

Sketches

What happens in the sketches is a result of the university biting the bullet and spending some money on building parking areas. There is a professor who was able to find parking and get to teach his class on time. There is also a student who was able to get to class on time because she was able to find an empty parking spot. Lastly is a university administrator who is furious with how much the extra parking cost to the university.

Affinity Diagram

The diagram shown offers 20 different ideas sectioned into 5 different categories showing how the solution of more parking availability can be attained.

Prototype

The app allows students and faculty members of the University of South Carolina to reserve a parking spot in a desired parking garage or parking lot. Once the space has been reserved, the app will provide directions to the designated parking spot. Students and faculty will be required to scan their Carolina Card to enter the respective garages and lots.
